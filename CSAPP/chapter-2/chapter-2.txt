from slot right to slot left

B2Tw
left term: isolate last bit on the left (MSB) at index w-1
right term: handle the remaining bits starting from right side (i=0)
up to w-2 (excluding the MSB)

example
4bit word size (w=4)

Tmax (highest possible val)
turn off the MSB and make the rest of the bit turn on

TMin = TMax + 1
as noted previously negative value has more 'range' from -128 to -1
